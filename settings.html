Your data is stored in this browser. If the browser data is cleared, your data will be lost. Always keep a backup!

📤 Export Backup

Downloads a .json file containing ALL your data (jobs, parts, technicians, suppliers, settings). Save this file on your computer, USB drive, or cloud storage.

📥 Import Backup

Restore your data from a previously exported .json backup file. Warning: This will replace ALL current data.

🚨 Danger Zone

This will permanently delete ALL your data — jobs, parts, technicians, suppliers, and settings. The system will reset to factory defaults. This cannot be undone!
